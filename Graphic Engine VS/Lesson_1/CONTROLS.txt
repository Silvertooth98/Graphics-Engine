					CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Movement:

W - Forward
A - Left
S - Backward
D - Right

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Camera:

Mouse Motion
Space - Up
Left_Control - Down

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Light Controls:

Up Arrow - Up
Left Arrow - Left
Down Arrow - Down
Right Arrow - Right
O - Forward
P - Backward
LI - Control one specific light only
LJ - Control the other specific light only
LK - Control all the lights in the scene (default option)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Light Interactivity:

Minus (-) - Turn light(s) off
	* if light(s) is turned off, controls for light don't work unless turned back on
Plus (+) - Turn light(s) on
1 - Lights turn Red and have ambient, diffuse and specular values for RUBY
2 - Lights turn Green and have   ""			       "" for EMERALD
3 - Lights turn Blue and have    ""			       "" for TURQUOISE
4 - Lights turn Yellow and have  ""			       "" for GOLD
5 - Lights turn Purple and have  ""			       "" for OBSIDIAN
6 - Lights turn Silver and have  ""			       "" for SILVER
7 - Lights turn White and have   ""			       "" for full white (0.5f)
